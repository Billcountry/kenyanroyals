Mission

Our mission as a community is to use the resources and skills that we have to build a better world for everyone. Our main focus is to ensure that everyone has a home and that they have the means to run and even grow their home. This range from orphans, individuals and families living on the street.

Vision

We envision a world where everyone has a place to call home, earns an honest living and everyone is self dependent as we all strive to build a better nation for the future.
It will be forever impossible only if we don't try.
